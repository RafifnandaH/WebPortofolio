Digital Manufacturing

Digital manufacturing is a method of production in which computer technology manufactures products in a desired style or quantity with little or no involvement from humans. The technology utilized in this approach offers companies the ability to develop prototypes and plan and customize the production process from start to finish. Integrating those variable to digital platform (IIoT) will make both human and machines performance more efficient, rapid, accurate, and errorless.
